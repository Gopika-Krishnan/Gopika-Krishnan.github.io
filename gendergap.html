Exploring Gender Gap

ECON-UB 232-002 Data Bootcamp (NYU Stern School of Business)
Fall 2020 Solo Project

I explored the different facets of how the gender gap represents itself using data that has information on companies from different industries and regions (inflow, outflow, job category, year joined). These facets included:
• Employment
• Region
• Sector
While doing this project, I focussed solely on the different insights one can derive from data and how data visualizations can be used as a medium to convey information by working on different types of graphs/color schemes/plots.
