    re:thought

    re:thought is a multimedia platform cofound by me and two of my friends from NYU Abu Dhabi aimed towards deconstructing social norms. We analyse how social norms came to be, why we follow them and how our lives are affected as we accept these norms at face value. We want to restart conversations that are often held and left behind closed doors and re:think out loud.
