Community involvement

Women Empowered in STEM (WeSTEM), NYU Abu Dhabi, Abu Dhabi, UAE


I am an advocate for diversity in the STEM community and thus, love being part of weSTEM. It is a student interest group (SIG) at NYUAD committed to empowering women, non-binary people, and male allies interested in Science Technology Engineering & Mathematics regardless of their major. I play/ed the following roles in the SIG:

Co-President


• Manage a team of 33 and advocate for diversity, equity, and inclusion across the institution

• Create a long-term vision of SIG and semester timeline of programming and initiatives

• Spearhead partnerships with other organizations on campus and across a global network

• Lead the mentorship program to facilitate networking among the first year and upper-class students.

Head of First-Year Programming, Jan 2021- Present


• Design and coordinate programming for first-year students (major panels, alum chats, mixers)

• Lead the mentorship program to facilitate networking among the first year and upper-class students.

Head of Operations - High School Conference Eboard, Sept 2020 - March 2021


• Contacted speakers and panelists to participate in the fifth edition of the conference (50-80 attendees)

• Managed a team of 2 to create a framework for the event to be engaging when transitioning online
